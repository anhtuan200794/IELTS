What are the benefits of studying aboardd?

Studying abroad is a dream of many students in VN.
If studying abroad you can have a better education as the result you will have more chances, more opportunities for the job when you finish your studying.
You can learn more and be a master of the second language as it is your circumstance. You will have more experience of the culture of the country that you move to. One more thing, you will have a good point in your Passport, it will be your advantage when you need to get a visa to travel aboard.
On the other hand, when studying abroad you have to face some trouble like culture shock, homesickness, or lack of money...
In my opinion, studying abroad will give you a brightening feature so if you have a chance to study aboard, let's do it!
